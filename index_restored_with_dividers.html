About Me

Alia Hall is an Assistant Professor of Communication at Daytona State College and a McKnight Doctoral Fellow in the Texts and Technology Ph.D. program at the University of Central Florida. With over fifteen years of technical industry experience and a strong background in higher education program management, her research interests focus on AI literacy, digital humanities, critical technocultural discourse, and inclusive pedagogical practices.
