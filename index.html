Writing Sample

Title: Race, Technology, and Trust — Examining the Cheating Discourse Among Black and Asian AI Users

Authors: Alia Hall and Yingzi Kong, University of Central Florida

This research examines cultural biases and assumptions of dishonesty experienced by Black and Asian students using AI in academia. It utilizes Critical Technocultural Discourse Analysis (CTDA) and feminist autoethnography to expose technocultural inequities and to advocate for culturally responsive AI pedagogy.
